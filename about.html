About Me

My Story

Hi I’m Laura, or as my friends call me, just “Blondi.” I’m a UI designer born and raised in Queens, NY. I have a passion for creating content that is flawless, easy to use, and improves people’s lives in one way or another. Sometimes the smallest design elements can have the biggest impact in our lives.

My journey with design has been ongoing and ever changing. I’ve always had a keen eye for the smallest details. From an early age I began immersing myself in different creative areas such painting, sculpture, and makeup. Throughout college I chose to study biology. While I loved learning and had a passion for science, I would often find myself drawing meticulous sketches and doodles instead of studying. As I moved into the workforce, I still could not shake the feeling that I was destined to do something that was creative and visually stimulating. This was when I made the leap to leap to move into the UI Design field.

My professional background in customer service has taught me how to successfully dedicate myself to a customer, and identify their needs to create solutions to problems. My most recent experience in the legal field let me collaborate with several different departments from bankruptcy to healthcare, which allowed me to become skilled in organizing different tasks and data. This experience coupled with my passion for creativity and design has helped me recognize what I am truly enthusiastic about, which is understanding people on a deeper level to create meaningful designs that make our lives better.

I look forward to continuing to learn and build my skills and passions to make the world a more positive place and design for companies who’s mission seeks to do the same. When I’m not creating, you can find me at a spin class, walking my dogs, or on the hunt for the best coffee in town.
